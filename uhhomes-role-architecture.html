1. All Possible User Roles in the Platform

Beyond just "Admin" and "User" — a mature home construction platform needs these 8 distinct roles, each with clear responsibilities.

🏠

Homebuyer / Owner

End Customer

The person who purchased the home. Tracks construction, views updates, asks questions, downloads documents.

  • View project dashboard & progress
  • Browse construction timeline
  • View photo/video gallery
  • Submit inquiries to builder
  • Receive alerts & notifications
  • Download documents (contracts, permits)
  • Favorite properties for comparison
  • Receive AI weekly digest email
  • View & pay invoices (future)
👑

Company Admin

Super Administrator

The construction company owner or top-level manager. Has full access to everything — creates roles, manages the platform.

  • Full CRUD on all entities
  • Create & manage user accounts
  • Assign roles (PM, Supervisor, etc.)
  • View company-wide analytics
  • Generate PDF reports
  • Manage properties catalog
  • Broadcast alerts to all buyers
  • Configure platform settings
  • Trigger AI summary generation
📋

Project Manager

Manages Projects

Assigned to one or more projects. Oversees milestones, coordinates between site supervisors and buyers, ensures timelines are met.

  • View & update assigned projects only
  • Create & manage milestones
  • Approve site updates before publishing
  • Respond to buyer inquiries
  • Generate project-level reports
  • Manage project documents
  • Set milestone target dates
  • Escalate delays to Admin
🔨

Site Supervisor / Foreman

On-Ground Updates

The person on the construction site. Takes daily photos, logs progress, reports issues. The primary source of real-time updates.

  • Post daily/weekly site updates
  • Upload photos & videos from site
  • Update milestone progress %
  • Log issues & delays
  • Mark inspection checkpoints
  • Limited to assigned projects
🤝

Sales Agent / Realtor

Customer Acquisition

Handles property inquiries from prospects, onboards new buyers, manages the pre-sale relationship until project assignment.

  • View property catalog
  • Manage prospect leads
  • Schedule property tours
  • Onboard new buyer accounts
  • Track inquiry-to-sale pipeline
  • View assigned buyer profiles
  • No access to construction data
📐

Architect / Designer

Design & Plans

Uploads floor plans, elevations, 3D renders. Handles design-related buyer requests like customizations or material selections.

  • Upload & manage floor plans
  • Upload elevation renders
  • Handle design change requests
  • View assigned projects
  • Comment on milestone updates
  • No access to financial data
💰

Finance / Accounts

Payments & Billing

Manages payment milestones, generates invoices, tracks buyer payments, handles financial reporting.

  • Create payment schedules
  • Generate & send invoices
  • Track payment status per buyer
  • Financial reports & summaries
  • Link payments to milestones
  • Send payment reminders
  • No access to construction updates
🔍

Inspector / QA

Quality Assurance

Conducts inspections at key milestones (foundation, framing, final). Approves or flags issues before project moves forward.

  • View milestone inspection checkpoints
  • Submit inspection reports
  • Approve / reject milestone completion
  • Upload inspection photos & notes
  • Flag violations or issues
  • Read-only access to project
